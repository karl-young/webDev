Trivia

    💡 Did You Know?

  • Pac-Man was originally going to be called “Puck-Man”
    but the name was changed for the American release to stop people from vandalizing the "P" into an "F" on the cabinets.
  • Street Fighter II was so popular that some arcade machines broke from overuse
    and arcades would line them with fans to cool them down.
  • The Donkey Kong arcade game was the first appearance of Mario
    but back then, he was just called “Jumpman.”
  • Galaga was one of the first arcade games to have a continue feature
    but only if you pressed start within a few seconds after dying.
  • Dance Dance Revolution became so intense that some arcades had to put up signs warning players not to play after eating!
  • Arcades were such a big deal in Japan that some machines had ashtrays built into them
    smoking while playing was totally normal.
